About Us

SeaVian, a leading International and Domestic Seafood Supplier and Producer, is dedicated to producing, sourcing, and facilitating the deliverance of high quality global fish and seafood to consumers worldwide. Having multiple international operations that range in live, fresh, or frozen seafood, we have established our brand in more than 10 countries of origin to over 30 destinations worldwide. SeaVian triumphs on having the ability of providing their clientele with their vision of providing what they believe to be the highest quality product with one of the most competitive prices on the market.

With Product safety being the main priority of SeaVian, we desire only exceptional compliance from agreed specifications that are guaranteed by the precise inspection by our suppliers from three different stages of the production process.

Our innovation and wide array of unique products define our signature style and ensure our ability to supply the clientele with new value-added products for both retail and food industries. We have partner-based access to several suppliers worldwide granting us the ability to offer vast range of seafood products and continue to seek out new opportunities and markets to allow consumers to be the first to have fresh new products in their marketplace.

With SeaVian’s quality and amazing customer service, we guarantee that our consumers will be completely satisfied with not only our products but with our business ventures as well!
